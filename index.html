Account Deletion and Data Removal

Permanently Deleting Your Account

We respect your privacy, and if you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data from our system, please follow the steps outlined below:

Initiating Account Deletion

1.1. Log in to your account on our web application.

1.2. Data UsageNavigate to the "Account Settings" or a similar section where account management options are available.

Locate Account Deletion Option

2.1.In the account settings, look for the "Delete Account" or a similar option.

2.2.Follow the on-screen instructions to initiate the account deletion process.

Confirmation and Verification

3.1.You may be asked to confirm your decision to delete the account.

3.2.For security purposes, additional verification steps may be required to ensure that the account owner is initiating the deletion.

Data Deletion Process

Once the account deletion is confirmed and verified, our system will begin the process of permanently removing all data associated with your account.

Important Information

Irreversible Process: Account deletion is irreversible. Once the process is complete, all data associated with your account will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
